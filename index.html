FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Krnl Executor free on PC?

Yes. The Windows version of Krnl Executor is completely free. Be wary of any site that asks for payment or account credentials — those are scams.

Do I need scripts for Krnl to work?

Yes. Krnl is only the executor — it does nothing on its own. You must paste, load or select scripts within the app. Some scripts require their own key system.

Does Krnl support Android or iOS?

Krnl has separate builds for Android, iOS and a special Vietnamese version (Krnl VNG). Those live on their own pages. This site is focused on the PC release.

Is using Krnl safe and allowed?

Using any exploit can violate Roblox's Terms of Service and may result in account penalties. Executors may also be flagged by antivirus software. Only download from official links and never use exploits on accounts you cannot afford to lose.

Krnl stopped working after an update. What now?

When Roblox updates, exploits often need time to catch up. Check if a newer Krnl build is available. If Krnl is down for a longer period, many users temporarily switch to alternative executors.
